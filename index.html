Speed Profile for Autonomous Vehicle

 

Revolutionizing autonomous pallet jack operations, I pioneered a dynamic speed profile algorithm that intelligently adjusts vehicle speed in real-time based on path curvature. Deployed successfully in ROS, C++, and Eigen, this innovation enhances safety standards, ensuring optimal performance and adaptability. By prioritizing safety through curvature-aware speed adjustments, the deployed solution not only elevates operational efficiency but also sets a new benchmark for intelligent and secure autonomous vehicle systems, showcasing my technical expertise and commitment to pushing the boundaries of autonomous vehicle technology.
