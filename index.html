Featured Projects

Speed Profile for Non-Holonomic Autonomous Vehicle

 

Revolutionizing autonomous pallet jack operations, I pioneered a dynamic speed profile algorithm that intelligently adjusts vehicle speed in real-time based on path curvature. Deployed successfully in ROS, C++, and Eigen, this innovation enhances safety standards, ensuring optimal performance and adaptability. By prioritizing safety through curvature-aware speed adjustments, the deployed solution not only elevates operational efficiency but also sets a new benchmark for intelligent and secure autonomous vehicle systems, showcasing my technical expertise and commitment to pushing the boundaries of autonomous vehicle technology.

Graph-Based Planners for Warehouse Navigation

I spearheaded the development of a cutting-edge graph-based planner tailored for warehouse navigation in autonomous vehicles, a breakthrough that not only streamlines fleet management but also significantly accelerates plan generation times. This iteration of the planner marks a paradigm shift by eliminating the need for a pre-existing map, showcasing its adaptability to dynamic environments. By seamlessly integrating this innovative solution, I have not only optimized operational efficiency but also demonstrated a commitment to advancing autonomous vehicle technology with a keen focus on versatility and time efficiency.

Navigation Stack for Outdoor Autonomous Tractor

Innovating agricultural automation, I designed a comprehensive coverage-based global path planner for autonomous tractors, revolutionizing farm cultivation. This solution optimizes tractor routes, ensuring thorough coverage of the farm. Complementing this, I engineered a local planner to precisely track the path and generate dynamic speed profiles, enhancing operational efficiency. To streamline the entire process, I developed a script enabling seamless execution of all modules, from embedded systems to perception, with a single click. This accomplishment reflects my holistic approach to advancing autonomous agricultural systems, seamlessly integrating global path planning, precise local maneuvering, and streamlined module execution. This cohesive strategy stands as a testament to my commitment to revolutionizing farming automation, ensuring efficiency and precision across every facet of autonomous tractor operations.
